# Curiosities Mod

A comprehensive Minecraft mod that adds a wide variety of unique items, enchantments, status effects, and gameplay
mechanics to enhance your Minecraft experience.

## ğŸŒŸ Features

### ğŸ—¡ï¸ Weapons & Tools

- **Rose Gold Tools** - Balanced tools with iron mining level, gold mining speed, and enhanced enchantability
- **Void Devourer Sword** - Powerful weapon that collects void energy and creates black holes
- **Lucky Sword** - Unpredictable weapon with random damage output (-15 to +30)
- **Probability Holy Sword** - Mysterious weapon with 20% chance for special effects
- **Staff of Control** - Control entities to fight each other
- **Scythes** - Efficient farming tools for bulk crop harvesting with auto-replanting
- **Rocket Boots** - Charge-jump boots with fall damage reduction

### ğŸ§ª Magical Items

- **Causal Pocket Watch** - Store and revert player states with time manipulation
- **Scroll of Spacetime** - Create anchors and teleport across dimensions
- **Entity Compass** - Track and highlight specific entity types
- **Time-Space Scroll** - Ancient magical scroll for instant travel

### ğŸ¯ Consumables & Utilities

- **Infinite Water Bucket** - Unlimited water source with 3x3 collection
- **Wolf Fang Potato** - Grants Wolf Pack Leader effect
- **Toxic Gland** - Dangerous food with multiple negative effects
- **Bee Grenade** - Throwable weapon that releases angry bees
- **Rich Potions** - Make villagers follow you with love effects
- **Undying Potions** - Trigger Totem of Undying effects

### âš¡ Enchantments

- **Chain Mining** - Mine connected blocks of the same type (Max Level 4)
- **Super Fortune** - 1.5x vanilla Fortune effect, compatible with Silk Touch (Max Level 3)
- **Moral Balance** - Different effects for peaceful vs hostile mobs
- **Steel Covenant** - Damage cap protection for chestplates (Max Level 5)
- **Proficiency** - Increases weapon attack speed (Max Level 4)

### ğŸŒ€ Status Effects

- **Wolf Pack Leader** - Nearby wolves assist in combat
- **Guilt** - Increases villager trade prices
- **Dizzy** - Reverses movement controls
- **Spinning** - Random camera rotation
- **Dissolving Body** - Reduces damage immunity time
- **Rich** - Attracts villagers with heart effects
- **Confusion** - Chance to redirect attacks to yourself
- **Undying** - Triggers totem effects on fatal damage

### ğŸ§± Blocks & Mechanics

## ğŸ® Getting Started

### Basic Crafting

Most items in Curiosities require advanced materials. Start by gathering:

- **Basic Materials**: Iron, Gold, Copper, Diamonds
- **Advanced Materials**: Netherite, Nether Stars, End Crystals
- **Special Items**: Totems of Undying, Dragon Heads, Various Mob Heads

### Configuration

The mod includes an extensive configuration system accessible through the mod menu:

- **Items Settings** - Enable/disable specific items and adjust their properties
- **Status Effects Settings** - Control effect durations and intensities
- **Enchantments Settings** - Modify enchantment availability and power
- **Blocks Settings** - Toggle block features and mechanics

### Key Recipes

```
Rose Gold Tools: Golden Tool + Copper Ingot (Smithing Table)
Entity Compass: Mob Heads + Compass + Leather + Feather
Causal Pocket Watch: Iron + Clock + Nether Stars
Infinite Water Bucket: Water Bucket surrounded by Prismarine Shards + Sponges
```
